WHAT IS DSC?

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) is a program presented by Google Developers. DSCs are university based community groups for students. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
Google does not own or manage DSCs. These are our communities.

DSC University Hassan 1 was started Nov 3, 2019 with the Help of its first leader (Abderrahman JAIZE) in order to impact the hassan 1 university students to grow their knowledge and build solution for themselves.

DSC ACTIVITIES

Over the span of the DSC, it is the responsibility of DSC club to help students interested in growing as developers learn and develop their skills. How we do that is up to the Google Developers ambassador by deploying Info sessions , workshops, events, courses, setting up speaker sessions, hackathons or focusing on building projects - it's up to The DSC lead to pick and choose what works for the student community.
( The leads are selected by Google )

WHAT WILL HAPPEN IN DSC?

Students will get to learn about various emerging technologies like Mobile and Web Development, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Cloud Platforms, etc.
Moreover, the number of workshops will be conducted by the University Innovation Fellows from across Morocco, encouraging students to find creative and innovative solutions, using advanced technologies to solve problems faced by local communities.
